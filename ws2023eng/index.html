General information

Dear colleagues,

Welcome to the International Networking-Forum “Effective Post-Covid EFL Digital Training: Skills, Tools and Techniques” held in the framework of annual Winter School of Pedagogical Excellence “Digitalization of Education and Language Education in Post-Covid Epoch: Trends and Technologies”.

Networking forum is a platform for building and strengthening effective communication between organizations, experts in language education and practitioners to share the experiences, scientific and methodological developments, to cooperate in science and teaching across Udmurtia, other regions of the Russian Federation and overseas countries, discussing digital didactics, linguodidactics and language education as essential components of vocational training today.

We hope this event will be an engaging and valuable experience for you.

Best regards,
Organizing Committee
